A short description of Geldermalsen can be found on https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geldermalsen

Geldermalsen is situated in the center of the netherlands and in the municipality the most important highway that connect Amsterdam to the South (A2) and the highway that connects the port of Rotterdam to Germany (A15) cross each other.
In the area, there is a lot of fruitcultivation (apples and pears).
